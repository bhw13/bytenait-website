Who We Are

We're growing alongside our technology
And our technology grows alongside you

BYTENAIT, derived from binate, meaning to grow in pairs, was founded by brothers Ben and Sam Wilt: a West Point Operations Research graduate and a machine learning engineer. The name reflects our core belief: the best technology and its users grow together.

It started with a problem. Preparing for the GRE, Ben found vocabulary tools antiquated and overpriced. He enlisted Sam, and together, they built Opticram: an adaptive, AI-powered vocabulary engine. Ben used it to score in the 95th percentile verbal and earn admission to Kellogg's MBAi program.

That result became a mission. BYTENAIT exists to build technology that helps people learn faster, perform better, and reach further than they could alone, starting with students, and growing from there.

Our Mission

Why we exist

To build technology that grows with its users. Intelligent tools that help people learn faster, think clearer, and do more than they thought possible.

Adaptive by Design

Our tools recalibrate around each person. They model where you are, identify what's holding you back, and surface exactly what you need — not a generic path, but yours.

Measurement Obsessed

Built on Operations Research. We track outcomes at a granular level, run statistical models on performance, and let data guide every product decision — not assumptions.

Access Without Compromise

The best tools have historically been priced out of reach. We believe powerful, personalized software should be available to anyone — not just those who can afford a premium.

Continuously Evolving

Great software doesn't ship once and stop. Our generation pipelines keep content fresh, our models keep learning, and our products keep improving — automatically, with every session.
